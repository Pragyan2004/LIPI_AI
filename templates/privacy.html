Data Ethics

Lipi AI operates under a strict "Sovereign In-Memory" protocol.

100% On-Shore Processing

In compliance with India's **Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023**, all linguistic processing is performed within local memory buffers. We do not store, relay, or monetize your creative expressions.

Collected Data
  • - Voluntarily added tokens
  • - Anonymized latency stats
  • - Current local session logs
Excluded Data
  • - Personal Identity Information
  • - Sensitive behavioral metrics
  • - External server relay data
Neural Training Consent

Unlike global LLMs, Lipi AI does not scrape your private inputs to improve its core weights. Training is conducted exclusively on public domain Indic corpora and verified research datasets.
